## 📖 Frequently asked questions

### Does this software change my original files?
No, the program reads data from your drive and writes a separate file. It does not alter your original documents or settings.

### How often should I run a backup?
Standard practice involves weekly backups for home users. If you change your files every day, perform daily backups.

### Can I save images to a cloud drive?
Yes, but consider the upload speed. Large images take significant time to transfer over an internet connection. External USB drives offer better performance.

### Does the software work during current use?
Yes, R-Drive Image creates images while Windows runs. You do not need to pause your tasks or restart the system to perform a backup.

## 💡 Best practices for backups

Keep at least three copies of your data. Store one copy on your computer, one on an external drive, and one in a different physical location. This strategy protects you against hardware failures or accidents. Update your images after you install major system updates or new programs. Test your restoration procedure once a year to ensure you understand the steps.
